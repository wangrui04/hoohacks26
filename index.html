⛏ Gold Rush — Game Rules ⛏

Each player takes turns making one move per turn.

Actions: Buy a mine, Buy a river, Upgrade an asset, or Skip turn.

Mines have a risk of collapsing — higher risk means higher reward.

Rivers yield lower rewards than mines but they never collapse.

Players cannot buy rivers consecutively — you must buy a mine before your next river.

Each asset can be upgraded once to increase its reward, which costs a turn.

A match ends when a player earns at least $500.

There are no limits to the number of turns per match.

A game consists of 5 matches.

A player needs to win 3 out of 5 matches to win the game.
